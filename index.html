Yes, we do customise any lehenga into an Anarkali and can change Anarkali into lehenga as well. You will have to mention it in the customisation box for every order

Do you have zip,Buttons customisation?

Yes we do, you just have to mention it in the customisation box while ordering, and our team will contact you for further assistance and additional payment of Rs. 200/-

Can you alter the length of my dress?

Yes we do alter the length of your dress according to your preference. You can also customise neck, sleeves, yoke as per your choice.
